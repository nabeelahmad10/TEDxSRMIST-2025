TEDxSRMIST

TEDxSRMIST is an independently organized TED event that aims to explore the boundless possibilities that lie beyond our earthly confines. Join us for a day of inspiring talks, innovative ideas, and a journey through the cosmos of human potential.

EMPYREAN : A VISION BEYOND THE STARS

Empyrean, the official theme for TEDxSRMIST, invites you to look beyond the horizon of human knowledge, feeling, thoughts and imagination. Join us on 1st April 2025, as we explore groundbreaking ideas that push the boundaries of what's possible and inspire us to reach for the stars.

WHY ATTEND TEDxSRMIST?

Knowledge is a gift meant to be shared. The more you give, the deeper it resonates within you, shaping new perspectives. Attending TEDxSRMIST is a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in groundbreaking ideas, thought-provoking discussions, and inspiring stories. It's a platform to gain insights from experts, challenge your thinking, and spark meaningful conversations. Whether you're a speaker or an audience member, the experience can ignite a lasting impact, fostering personal growth and a shift in perspective that stays with you long after the event.
